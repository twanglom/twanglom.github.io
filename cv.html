Professional Summary

PhD candidate in Mechanical Engineering at École Centrale de Lyon with expertise in optimization under uncertainty, machine learning, and high-frequency vibroacoustic simulation. Specialized in developing innovative methods for noise reduction, uncertainty quantification, and shape optimization for applications such as aircraft cabin design. Strong background in control systems, encompassing dynamics and system modeling, designing and deploying embedded control systems. Created multiple software solutions including PyEGRO for Robust Design Optimization, an open-source Python library for efficient global optimization under uncertainty. Experienced in teaching, industrial training, and software development, with a focus on integrating AI-driven solutions into engineering research.
